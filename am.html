Keep San Ramon Natural!

As part of an outreach initiative by Aparna Madireddi, a candidate for San Ramon Mayor in the upcoming November Elections, we have put together a website to show how you can help San Ramon maintain its uniqueness, and beauty. This website is for creating awareness within the community about projects that are going on that may ruin the natural beaty of San Ramon, and change its open space environment that we have come to love.
